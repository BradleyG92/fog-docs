==DHCP Settings==
*It is important to know that versions 0.32 and below use '''pxelinux.0''' for option 67 in DHCP
*For all versions 0.33 to current(1.3.0beta) use '''undionly.kpxe''' for option 67.
** Other files that can be used are  undionly.kkpxe


===FOG internal DHCP===
#'''FOG 0.1-->0.32'''
#*Go to your DHCP and confirm that option 67 is set to '''pxelinux.0'''
#'''Fog 0.33-->1.x.x'''
#*Go to your DHCP and confirm that option 67 is set to '''undionly.kpxe'''
